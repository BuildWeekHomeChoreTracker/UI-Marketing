Welcome to Home Chore Tracker

Welcome to Home Chore Tracker! This innovative new app provides you, the parent, the ability to take back control of the house and make sure the kids are doing their part to keep it clean and tidy. If you have exhausted all of your efforts to ward off high allowance demands on top of low quality work, then you must download this app and start taking back control of your home. Home Chore Tracker will allow you to do just that by providing you with a ‘gamified’ approach to home chore tracking and management.

Excuses Excuses...

We have all heard the countless excuses:

  • "I forgot!"
  • "I thought it was clean enough!
  • "Why do I have to do chores in the first place? Isn't that what the maid is for?"

and my personal favorite...


  • "It's my room, I can keep it any way that I want. You have no control over what goes on behind my door."

Enough is enough! With Home Chore Tracker, excuses will be a thing of the past. Chore Challenge is an easy way to get the kids excited about keeping the home clean and orderly. You can create a list of chores yourself, or use one of our templates, then assign them to your children.

Let the Cleaning Begin

With Chore Score, let's see who in your family can stay on top the longest!
